# Key Points - 关键观点

> 来源: sample-lesson.srt
> 主题: 环境保护与气候变化
> 目标分数: IELTS 7.0

---

## 1. Climate change is one of the most pressing issues of our time
**中文**: 气候变化是我们这个时代最紧迫的问题之一

**用法**: 用于引入环境类话题的开篇陈述

**例句扩展**:
- Climate change stands as one of the most pressing issues of our time, demanding immediate global attention.
- Ranked among the most pressing issues of our time, climate change requires urgent action.

---

## 2. Human activities account for most of the global warming
**中文**: 人类活动是造成全球变暖的主要原因

**用法**: 表达因果关系，account for 表示"是……的原因"

**例句扩展**:
- Industrial activities account for approximately 60% of global carbon emissions.
- Human activities account for the vast majority of environmental degradation observed in recent decades.

---

## 3. Innovative solutions are needed to address this challenge
**中文**: 需要创新的解决方案来应对这一挑战

**用法**: 提出解决问题的必要性

**例句扩展**:
- To address this challenge effectively, innovative solutions combining technology and policy are essential.
- We must develop innovative solutions to address the growing challenge of resource scarcity.

---

## 4. Governments should implement policies based on renewable energy
**中文**: 政府应实施基于可再生能源的政策

**用法**: 提出政策建议

**例句扩展**:
- Governments should implement policies that incentivize the adoption of renewable energy sources.
- Nations must implement policies that accelerate the transition to renewable energy infrastructure.

---

## 5. Individuals can contribute through sustainable daily choices
**中文**: 个人可以通过日常生活中的可持续选择做出贡献

**用法**: 强调个人责任

**例句扩展**:
- Individuals can contribute significantly to environmental protection through sustainable daily choices.
- Every individual can contribute to the collective effort through conscious, sustainable daily choices.

---

## 6. Education plays a crucial role in raising awareness
**中文**: 教育在提高意识方面发挥着关键作用

**用法**: 强调某事物的重要性

**例句扩展**:
- Environmental education plays a crucial role in raising awareness among younger generations.
- Comprehensive education plays a crucial role in raising awareness about sustainability issues.

---

## 7. Teaching environmental science in schools is necessary
**中文**: 在学校教授环境科学是必要的

**用法**: 提出教育改革建议

**例句扩展**:
- Teaching environmental science in schools is necessary to foster long-term behavioral change.
- Integrating environmental science into school curricula is necessary for building an informed citizenry.

---

## 8. Collective action can make a significant difference
**中文**: 集体行动可以产生重大影响

**用法**: 总结性陈述，呼吁合作

**例句扩展**:
- Through collective action, communities can make a significant difference in local environmental outcomes.
- When nations work in unity, collective action can make a significant difference for future generations.

---

## IELTS Task 2 应用建议

**引入段 (Introduction)**:
- 使用 Point 1 引入话题

**主体段 (Body Paragraphs)**:
- Point 2 用于原因分析
- Point 3-4 用于解决方案论证
- Point 5 用于个人层面论证
- Point 6-7 用于教育层面论证

**结论段 (Conclusion)**:
- 使用 Point 8 进行总结呼吁

**关联逻辑词**: Consequently, Furthermore, Additionally, Moreover, In conclusion
