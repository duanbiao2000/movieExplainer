# Verb Phrases - 动词短语

> 来源: sample-lesson.srt
> 主题: 环境保护与气候变化
> 目标分数: IELTS 7.0

---

## 1. conduct studies
**中文**: 进行研究
**原文**: Scientists around the world have **conducted** comprehensive **studies**.

**语法结构**: verb + noun
**时态变化**: conduct - conducted - conducted

**用法说明**:
- 正式学术搭配，可用于替换 "do research"
- 适用于描述科学研究、调查、分析

**例句**:
- Researchers have **conducted extensive studies** on the effects of pollution.
- The university **conducted a study** to examine student learning patterns.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 引用研究或数据时使用
- Academic Writing: 描述研究方法

**同义替换**:
- carry out research
- undertake investigation
- perform analysis

## 2. account for
**中文**: 是……的原因；占（比例）
**原文**: Human activities **account for** most of the global warming.

**语法结构**: phrasal verb (及物)
**时态变化**: account for - accounted for - accounted for

**用法说明**:
- 高分短语动词，解释原因或占比
- 用于建立因果关系
- 可表示"占据（比例）"

**例句**:
- Deforestation **accounts for** approximately 15% of global carbon emissions.
- Several factors **account for** the decline in biodiversity.
- Technology **accounts for** a significant portion of the budget.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 分析原因时使用（高频！）
- Academic Writing: 因果关系论述

**注意**: 不要与 "account of" 混淆

## 3. come up with
**中文**: 想出；提出
**原文**: We need to **come up with** innovative solutions.

**语法结构**: phrasal verb (不及物)
**时态变化**: come up with - came up with - come up with

**用法说明**:
- 口语和写作通用
- 表示提出想法、解决方案、计划
- 非正式到半正式语域

**例句**:
- The committee must **come up with** a strategy to reduce costs.
- Students need to **come up with** creative solutions to the problem.
- Scientists are trying to **come up with** a vaccine.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 提出解决方案时使用
- Speaking Part 3: 讨论问题时使用

**同义替换**:
- develop / devise / create / generate

## 4. address a challenge
**中文**: 应对挑战
**原文**: ...solutions to **address** this **challenge**.

**语法结构**: verb + noun
**时态变化**: address - addressed - addressed

**用法说明**:
- 正式搭配，可替换 "deal with" 或 "handle"
- 适用于问题解决场景
- 比简单的 "solve" 更正式

**例句**:
- The government must **address the challenge** of unemployment.
- New policies were introduced to **address environmental challenges**.
- We need a comprehensive approach to **address this challenge**.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 讨论问题和解决方案
- Academic Writing: 正式语域

**同义替换**:
- tackle a problem
- confront an issue
- overcome a difficulty

## 5. implement policies
**中文**: 实施政策
**原文**: Governments should **implement policies** that rely on renewable energy.

**语法结构**: verb + noun
**时态变化**: implement - implemented - implemented

**用法说明**:
- 政府类话题常用搭配
- 表示将政策付诸实施
- 正式语境

**例句**:
- The administration plans to **implement new policies** on taxation.
- It takes time to **implement** education **policies** effectively.
- Successful countries **implement policies** that balance growth and sustainability.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 政府类话题、解决方案类话题
- 适合 "government responsibility" 类题目

**同义替换**:
- enforce regulations
- apply measures
- execute programs

## 6. rely on
**中文**: 依赖；依靠
**原文**: ...policies that **rely on** renewable energy sources.

**语法结构**: phrasal verb
**时态变化**: rely on - relied on - relied on

**用法说明**:
- 表示依赖某事物
- 可与 "depend on" 互换
- 常用于描述策略或方法

**例句**:
- Modern societies **rely on** technology for communication.
- We should **rely on** renewable energy sources instead of fossil fuels.
- The economy cannot continue to **rely on** traditional industries.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 讨论依赖关系、策略选择
- 适合科技、环境、经济类话题

**同义替换**:
- depend on
- count on
- be dependent on

## 7. contribute to
**中文**: 为……做出贡献；导致
**原文**: Individuals can also **contribute to** by making sustainable choices.

**语法结构**: phrasal verb
**时态变化**: contribute to - contributed to - contributed to

**用法说明**:
- 表达积极作用或因果关系
- 既可表示"贡献"也可表示"导致"
- 根据语境判断褒贬义

**例句**:
- Regular exercise can **contribute to** better health. (积极)
- Pollution **contributes to** climate change. (中性/消极)
- Everyone should **contribute to** community development. (积极)

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 论述个人/集体的作用
- 因果关系分析

**同义替换**:
- help achieve / lead to / play a part in

## 8. raise awareness
**中文**: 提高意识
**原文**: ...education plays a crucial role in **raising awareness**.

**语法结构**: verb + noun
**时态变化**: raise - raised - raised

**用法说明**:
- 环境、健康、社会类话题常用
- 表示让更多人了解某问题
- 固定搭配

**例句**:
- Campaigns aim to **raise awareness** about mental health.
- Schools should **raise awareness** of environmental issues.
- Social media can help **raise awareness** effectively.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 教育类、公共健康类、环境类话题
- 适合 "education" 和 "media" 相关题目

**同义替换**:
- increase awareness
- promote understanding
- generate consciousness

## 9. due to ✨ NEW
**中文**: 由于；因为
**原文**: This trend is largely **due to** increased access to information.

**语法结构**: prepositional phrase
**用法说明**:
- 表示因果关系
- 比 "because of" 更正式
- 常用于学术写作

**例句**:
- The project's success was **due to** effective teamwork.
- Climate change effects are **due to** human activities.
- Flight cancellations were **due to** adverse weather conditions.

**IELTS 应用**:
- Task 2: 因果关系论述（高频！）
- Academic Writing: 正式语域

**同义替换**:
- because of / owing to / as a result of / attributable to

## 练习建议

### 1. 造句练习
使用每个动词短语造3个不同场景的句子

### 2. 改写练习
- Simple: "The government made new rules."
- Upgrade: "The government **implemented policies**..."

### 3. 实战应用
在Task 2写作中强制使用至少3个目标动词短语

### 4. 搭配扩展
为每个动词短语找到2个以上的名词搭配

## 优先级分级

### 🔴 一级优先（必须掌握）
- **account for** - Task 2 解释原因必备
- **come up with** - 解决方案类高频
- **due to** - 因果关系必备

### 🟡 二级优先（重要提升）
- **address a challenge** - 正式语域提升
- **implement policies** - 政府类话题
- **raise awareness** - 教育媒体类

### 🟢 三级优先（扩展应用）
- **conduct studies** - 学术写作
- **rely on** - 依赖关系
- **contribute to** - 多功能表达
