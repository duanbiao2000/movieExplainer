## 1. The evidence suggests that...
**中文**: 有证据表明……

**句型结构**: Noun + verb + that-clause
**语法**: 被动意味的客观陈述

**原文**:
> The **evidence suggests that** human activities account for most of the global warming.

**用法说明**:
- 学术写作中客观表达观点
- 避免使用 "I think" 或主观表达
- 引出研究结果或数据支持的观点
- 适用于 Task 2 主体段论证

**例句扩展**:
- **The evidence suggests that** early intervention yields better outcomes.
- **The evidence suggests that** corporal punishment has negative effects on child development.
- **The evidence suggests that** renewable energy is becoming increasingly cost-effective.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Introduction: Present the topic
Body Paragraph 1: The evidence suggests that [first argument]...
Body Paragraph 2: The evidence suggests that [second argument]...
```

**高分价值**: 提升客观性和学术性 (LR 7.0+)

## 2. A compelling case can be made for...
**中文**: 有充分理由支持……

**句型结构**: Indefinite article + adj. + noun + passive verb + preposition
**语法**: 被动语态 + 名词化表达

**原文**:
> A **compelling case can be made for** teaching environmental science in schools.

**用法说明**:
- 提出论证的高分表达
- 名词化提升正式度
- 适用于提出观点或建议
- 比 "I strongly believe that..." 更正式

**例句扩展**:
- **A compelling case can be made for** banning single-use plastics.
- **A compelling case can be made for** investing in public transportation.
- **A compelling case can be made for** reducing the working week to four days.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Body Paragraph:
A compelling case can be made for [proposal]. Firstly, [reason 1]. Furthermore, [reason 2]. Most importantly, [reason 3].
```

**高分价值**: 名词化 + 被动语态 = 7.5+ 表达

## 3. This trend is largely due to...
**中文**: 这一趋势很大程度上是因为……

**句型结构**: Subject + verb + adverb + prepositional phrase
**语法**: due to 引导原因状语

**原文**:
> **This trend is largely due to** increased access to information about climate issues.

**用法说明**:
- 解释原因的正式表达
- "largely" 提供了精确的程度修饰
- 适用于分析趋势、现象的原因
- 比 "because of" 更学术

**例句扩展**:
- **This trend is largely due to** changing social attitudes.
- **This trend is largely due to** advances in medical technology.
- **This trend is largely due to** economic globalization.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Describe a trend → Explain causes:
This trend is largely due to [primary cause]. Additionally, [secondary factor] has also played a role.
```

**高分价值**: 精确程度修饰 + 正式因果表达 (LR 7.0+)

## 4. It is widely argued that...
**中文**: 人们普遍认为……

**句型结构**: Empty subject + passive verb + adverb + that-clause
**语法**: 形式主语 + 被动语态

**原文**:
> **It is widely argued that** education plays a crucial role in raising awareness.

**用法说明**:
- 引出普遍观点，避免主观性
- 虚拟主语 "It" 使句子更客观
- 适用于引出对立观点或普遍认知
- 可作为转折或让步的起点

**例句扩展**:
- **It is widely argued that** technology isolates rather than connects people.
- **It is widely argued that** globalization benefits developed nations more than developing ones.
- **It is widely argued that** traditional skills are being lost.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Introduction / Body:
It is widely argued that [opposing view]. However, this perspective overlooks [counter-argument].
```

**高分价值**: 避免主观性 + 设置论证层次 (LR 7.0+)

## 5. By working together, we can...
**中文**: 通过共同努力，我们可以……

**句型结构**: Preposition + gerund + subject + modal verb + infinitive
**语法**: By + doing 表示手段/方法

**原文**:
> **By working together**, we can make a significant difference for future generations.

**用法说明**:
- 表手段与结果的复杂句型
- 强调合作、共同努力
- 适用于结论段或呼吁段落
- 表达积极、建设性的态度

**例句扩展**:
- **By working together**, communities can create sustainable solutions.
- **By combining our efforts**, we can achieve remarkable progress.
- **By collaborating across borders**, nations can tackle global challenges effectively.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Conclusion:
In conclusion, by working together, governments, organizations, and individuals can [positive outcome].
```

**高分价值**: 复杂句型 + 建设性结论 (GRA 7.0+)

## 6. ...plays a crucial role in...
**中文**: ……在……中发挥关键作用

**句型结构**: Subject + verb + article + adj. + noun + preposition
**语法**: 固定搭配 "play a role in"

**原文**:
> Education **plays a crucial role in** raising awareness.

**用法说明**:
- 强调某事物的重要性
- "crucial" 比简单 "important" 更高级
- 适用于分析影响因素
- 非常高频的学术表达

**例句扩展**:
- Technology **plays a crucial role in** modern education.
- Parents **play a crucial role in** child development.
- Diet **plays a crucial role in** disease prevention.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Body Paragraph:
[Factor] plays a crucial role in [outcome]. For instance, [example]. Moreover, [further explanation].
```

**变体**:
- plays a vital role in...
- plays an essential role in...
- plays a significant role in...
- plays a key role in...

**高分价值**: 高级形容词 + 学术搭配 (LR 7.0+)

## 7. Scientists around the world have conducted comprehensive studies ✨ NEW
**中文**: 全球科学家已进行过全面研究

**句型结构**: Subject + location + verb + object
**语法**: 现在完成时 + 复杂名词短语

**原文扩展**:
> **Scientists around the world have conducted comprehensive studies** to understand climate change causes.

**用法说明**:
- 引用权威研究
- "around the world" 强调全球共识
- "comprehensive studies" 显示研究的全面性
- 适用于支持论点

**例句扩展**:
- **Scientists around the world have conducted comprehensive studies** on vaccine efficacy.
- **Researchers around the world have conducted extensive studies** regarding the impacts of social media.
- **Experts around the world have carried out comprehensive studies** on renewable energy viability.

**IELTS Task 2 应用**:
```
Body Paragraph:
Scientists around the world have conducted comprehensive studies on [topic]. The evidence consistently shows that [finding].
```

**高分价值**: 权威引用 + 全球视角 + 学术词汇 (LR 7.5+)

## 句型功能分类

### 📊 论证类 (Argumentation)
- The evidence suggests that... (客观论证)
- A compelling case can be made for... (提出观点)
- It is widely argued that... (引出观点)

### 🔍 分析类 (Analysis)
- This trend is largely due to... (原因分析)
- ...plays a crucial role in... (因素分析)

### 📝 引用类 (Citation)
- Scientists around the world have conducted comprehensive studies... (权威引用)

### 💡 建议呼吁类 (Recommendation)
- By working together, we can... (建设性呼吁)

## 练习建议

### 1. 句型转换
```
基础: People think education is important.
→ 一级: Many people argue that education plays an important role.
→ 高分: It is widely argued that education plays a crucial role in social development.
```

### 2. 段落应用
使用至少2个高分表达构建一个Task 2段落

### 3. 主题扩展
将这些表达应用到不同话题（教育、科技、健康等）

### 4. 对比练习
对比使用简单表达和高分表达的效果差异
