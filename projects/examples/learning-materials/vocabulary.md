# Vocabulary - 词汇表

> 来源: sample-lesson.srt
> 主题: 环境保护与气候变化
> 目标分数: IELTS 7.0

---

## Topic Vocabulary - 环境类主题词汇

### 1. pressing issues
**词性**: adj. + n.
**中文**: 紧迫问题
**例句**: Climate change is one of the most **pressing issues** of our time.
**同义词**: urgent matters, critical problems
**雅思应用**: Task 2 开头段引入话题

---

### 2. climate change
**词性**: n. phrase
**中文**: 气候变化
**例句**: The evidence suggests that **climate change** is accelerating.
**搭配**: address / tackle / mitigate climate change
**雅思应用**: 环境类话题核心词汇

---

### 3. global warming
**词性**: n. phrase
**中文**: 全球变暖
**例句**: Human activities account for most of the **global warming**.
**搭配**: cause / contribute to / exacerbate global warming
**雅思应用**: 因果关系论述

---

### 4. renewable energy
**词性**: n. phrase
**中文**: 可再生能源
**例句**: Policies should rely on **renewable energy** sources.
**搭配**: transition to / invest in / promote renewable energy
**雅思应用**: 解决方案类话题

---

### 5. sustainable choices
**词性**: adj. + n.
**中文**: 可持续选择
**例句**: Individuals can contribute by making **sustainable choices**.
**搭配**: make / adopt sustainable choices
**雅思应用**: 个人责任类论证

---

### 6. environmental science
**词性**: n. phrase
**中文**: 环境科学
**例句**: Teaching **environmental science** in schools is crucial.
**搭配**: study / research environmental science
**雅思应用**: 教育类话题

---

## Academic Vocabulary - 学术词汇

### 1. comprehensive
**词性**: adj.
**中文**: 全面的；详尽的
**例句**: Scientists have conducted **comprehensive** studies.
**搭配**: comprehensive study / analysis / review
**替换词**: thorough, complete, extensive
**雅思应用**: 描述研究或分析 (LR 7.0+)

---

### 2. innovative
**词性**: adj.
**中文**: 创新的
**例句**: We need **innovative** solutions.
**搭配**: innovative approach / solution / method
**替换词**: creative, novel, inventive
**雅思应用**: 描述解决方案 (LR 7.0+)

---

### 3. crucial
**词性**: adj.
**中文**: 至关重要的
**例句**: Education plays a **crucial** role.
**搭配**: crucial role / factor / element
**替换词**: vital, essential, critical
**雅思应用**: 表达重要性 (LR 7.0+)

---

### 4. compelling
**词性**: adj.
**中文**: 令人信服的
**例句**: A **compelling** case can be made.
**搭配**: compelling argument / evidence / reason
**替换词**: convincing, persuasive, cogent
**雅思应用**: 表达论证强度 (LR 7.5+)

---

### 5. significant
**词性**: adj.
**中文**: 显著的；重要的
**例句**: Make a **significant** difference.
**搭配**: significant impact / change / improvement
**替换词**: considerable, substantial, noteworthy
**雅思应用**: 表达程度和影响 (LR 7.0+)

---

## Collocations - 固定搭配

### 1. conduct comprehensive studies
**中文**: 进行全面研究
**结构**: verb + adj. + noun
**替换**: carry out research / undertake investigation
**雅思应用**: 学术写作 - 描述研究方法

---

### 2. make sustainable choices
**中文**: 做出可持续选择
**结构**: verb + adj. + noun
**扩展**: make eco-friendly choices / make ethical choices
**雅思应用**: 个人行为类话题

---

### 3. play a crucial role
**中文**: 发挥关键作用
**结构**: verb + article + adj. + noun
**变体**: play a vital role / play an important role
**雅思应用**: 表达重要性 - 高频句型

---

### 4. make a compelling case
**中文**: 提出令人信服的理由
**结构**: verb + article + adj. + noun
**扩展**: present a strong case / build a convincing argument
**雅思应用**: 议论文论证 - 高分表达

---

### 5. make a significant difference
**中文**: 产生重大影响
**结构**: verb + article + adj. + noun
**变体**: have a considerable impact / exert substantial influence
**雅思应用**: 表达效果和影响
