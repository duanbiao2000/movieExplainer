## 短语动词 (Phrasal Verbs)

### 囚禁与逃脱

| 短语动词 | 含义 | 例句 | 同义词 |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **lock up** | 关押，锁住 | "locked him in a prison cell" | imprison, confine |
| **break out** | 越狱，逃脱 | (implied in escape) | escape from prison |
| **let out** | 释放 | (opening the cell door) | release |
| **keep in** | 关在里面 | "locked alone in the cell" | confine |
| **seal off** | 封闭 | "quickly sealed the tunnel's entrance" | block, close |
| **break into** | 闯入 | (prison break context) | enter forcefully |
| **spring from** | 突然出现 | "man coming out from under the ground" | emerge from |

## 高级句型结构

### 条件句

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **If...would have...** | "If you had done something, you would have been sent to a jail within the country." |
| **Only those...are...** | "Only those against whom a conspiracy has been made are brought to this remote prison." |
| **If...can/will be able to** | "If Jacob won, he could rejoin the pirate crew." |

### 原因与结果

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **Because of this...** | "Because of the damage to his lungs, he could die at any moment." |
| **This caused...** | "The shock of it caused the warden to fall into the sea." |
| **As a result** | (implied consequences) |

### 时间表达

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **After + -ing** | "After spending 12 years in that prison" |
| **When + clause** | "When he realized he had no control" |
| **As soon as** | "As soon as he opened one of them" |
| **Before + -ing** | (action sequence) |
| **While + -ing** | "While the warden was trying to listen" |

### 让步与对比

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **Even** | "Even he couldn't understand how he was still alive" |
| **Despite** | "Despite several attacks, Yakob couldn't even touch Edmund" |
| **Although** | (implied concessions) |
| **However** | "However, when Mercedes saw him, she had a strange feeling" |
| **But instead of** | "But instead of Edmund, he found Abbe's body there" |

### 目的与意图

| 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| **in order to** | "in order to catch the attention" |
| **so that** | (purpose clauses) |
| **with the intention of** | (planning) |
| **aiming to** | (targeting) |

## 谓语动词模式

| 动词 | 模式 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **see** | see + 宾语 + 分词 | "see plant roots in the tunnel" |
| **hear** | hear + 名词/从句 | "hear the sound of footsteps" |
| **realize** | realize + that 从句 | "realize they were very close" |
| **notice** | notice + 名词 | "notice that Edmund had become" |
| **observe** | observe + 名词/从句 | (formal contexts) |

## 状态动词与动作动词

### 状态动词 (不能用于进行时)

| 动词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **be** | 是 | "was on an island" |
| **have** | 有 | "had a wooden door" |
| **know** | 知道 | "knew someone who could help" |
| **believe** | 相信 | "believed he could change his future" |
| **understand** | 理解 | "understand how he was still alive" |
| **seem** | 似乎 | "seemed to have the upper hand" |
| **appear** | 出现/似乎 | "appeared as his savior" |

| **fight** | 战斗 | "fight between the two began" |

| **chase** | 追逐 | "Edmund chased him" |
