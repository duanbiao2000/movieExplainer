## Task 2 作文结构

### 引言模式 (Introduction Patterns)

| 模式 | 应用示例 |
|------|----------|
| **The narrative explores...** | "The narrative explores complex themes of betrayal and revenge..." |
| **This story highlights...** | "This story highlights the moral complexity of seeking vengeance..." |
| **A key theme in...is...** | "A key theme in the story is the transformation of identity..." |
| **The plot centers on...** | "The plot centers on Edmund's journey from victim to avenger..." |

### 论点陈述 (Thesis Statement Patterns)

| 模式 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| **This essay will examine...** | 学术作文标准 |
| **This paper will argue that...** | 议论文 |

## 论证语言 (Argumentation Language)

### 同意 (Agreeing)

| 表达 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| **I concur that...** | 正式同意 |
| **I am inclined to agree that...** | 有保留的同意 |
| **There is merit to the argument that...** | 部分同意 |

### 不同意 (Disagreeing)

| 表达 | 用途 |
|------|------|

| **It could be contended that...** | 替代观点 |
| **This perspective overlooks...** | 批判性反对 |

|------|------|
