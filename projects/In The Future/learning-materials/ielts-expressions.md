## Task 2 Essay Structures

| Pattern | Example Application |
|---------|---------------------|
| **The narrative/film explores...** | "The film explores complex themes of..." |
| **This story highlights the issue of...** | "This story highlights the moral dilemma of..." |
| **A key theme in...is...** | "A key theme in the narrative is..." |
| **The plot centers on...** | "The plot centers on the concept of..." |

### Thesis Statement Patterns

| Pattern | Example Application |
|---------|---------------------|
| **This essay will examine...** | Academic essay standard |
| **This paper will argue that...** | Argumentative essay |
| **The following discussion will explore...** | Formal discussion |
