## 短语动词 (Phrasal Verbs)

### 数据处理

| 短语动词 | 含义 | 例句 | 同义词 |
|---------|------|------|--------|
| **pull from** | 从...提取 | "pull the data from the spreadsheets" | extract from |
| **gather from** | 从...收集 | "start gathering data from different sources" | collect from |
| **query** | 查询 | "query the different databases" | inquire into |
| **connect to** | 连接到 | "connect to multiple source systems" | link to |
| **flow into** | 流入 | "data going to flow from the sources" | stream into |
| **flow through** | 流经 | "flows through the different layers" | pass through |
| **base on** | 基于 | "decision process is based on opinions" | founded on |
| **rely on** | 依赖 | "They rely on opinions" | depend on |
| **lead to** | 导致 | "lead to confusion, wasted resources" | cause, result in |
| **result in** | 导致 | "making bad decisions for the business" | cause, lead to |

## 时态与体

### 一般现在时
- 用于描述常规工作流程
- 表达普遍真理
- 说明系统架构

### 现在进行时
- 描述正在发生的动作
- (implied throughout)

### 一般将来时
- "will be" (预测)
| "going to" (计划)
| 表达未来趋势和预测

### 完成时
- (implied in past actions)
